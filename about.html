The Founder

How it all Started

I was inspired to start this company by my eldest dog, Pops. His entire life he has had allergies to all kibble, wet or dry. I spent so much money on meds, ointments, and vet visits with little to no effect. Once I got tired of researching vets, I started researching different diets. Once I tried a raw diet the impact was immediate. He went from 92 lbs to 80 lbs in one month. I then learned about an ancestral diet and how important veggies and fruit were to canine's diet. Pops lost an additional 10 lbs the following month. Over the last 6 years I have been formulating raw diets for my canines and for other dog lovers near and far, culminating into what I believe is the best diet plan for your canine no matter the breed, age, weight or gender. My vision is to create a community of health conscious canine owners.
